\section{Reproducibility Notes}
\label{app:reproducibility}

The complete workflow and machine-readable outputs are retained in the public repository. The 20-seed predictive analysis is implemented in \texttt{paper1\_leakage\_benchmark/scripts/robustness\_20seeds.py}; the 5,000-draw random-scaffold audit is implemented in \texttt{balanced\_split\_null\_audit.py}; and the paired robustness figure is generated by \texttt{plot\_robustness20\_figure.py}. The corresponding raw, paired, summary, diagnostic, null-assignment, and percentile tables are stored under \texttt{paper1\_leakage\_benchmark/results/tables/}. Fixed fingerprints, hyperparameters, seed values, and output names are encoded in the scripts so that the reported results can be regenerated without manual intervention.
